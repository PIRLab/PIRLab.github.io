Build physical intelligence with us.

PIRLab welcomes motivated students and collaborators who want to work on robotics, computer vision, world models, and physically grounded action.

Openings

We are open to PhD, MPhil, research assistant, and visiting student applications.

Strong candidates typically bring experience in robotics, computer vision, 3D learning, world models, reinforcement learning, controls, or real robot systems.

The lab environment is a good fit for people who like both theory and implementation and who want research to stay close to deployable robotic capability.

What to Send

A concise email with your CV, transcripts, representative projects or papers, and a short note about the research direction you want to pursue.

What Helps

Evidence of strong coding ability, mathematical maturity, publication potential, or hands-on robotics experience.

Where It Can Lead

Formerly mentored students have continued to leading universities and labs including UC Berkeley, Princeton, HKU, Columbia, Georgia Tech, UCSD, UCLA, TUM, and SJTU.
